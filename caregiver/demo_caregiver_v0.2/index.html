Important information
How to read the estimates in this app.
⚠ Placeholder copy — pending legal review (OI-B-08). Final wording to be confirmed before production.
Estimates, not advice. Scheme eligibility and runway figures are estimates based on the information you enter and publicly available government data. They are not financial advice and do not guarantee any grant, payout, or outcome.

Verify before you act. Always confirm eligibility and amounts with the relevant agency — AIC (aic.sg), CPF Board (cpf.gov.sg), or MOH (moh.gov.sg) — before making decisions.

Your data stays on your device. Profile and financial details are stored locally. Only anonymised, aggregated figures are used to compute your runway — no names, NRIC, age, or health details leave your device.
