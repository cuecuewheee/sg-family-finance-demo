CareShield Life
Monthly disability payouts when a person cannot perform 3 or more Activities of Daily Living
S$600 – S$1,200+ / month
Amount depends on policy year and supplements
ℹ️ Informational onlyThis screen provides general information about CareShield Life. We do not assess or confirm your father's claim eligibility — please check with CPF Board directly.
How it works
🔔
Payouts begin when the insured person cannot perform 3 or more ADLs — the same threshold as HCG eligibility
📅
Automatic enrolment for Singapore Citizens and PRs born 1980 or later. Older cohorts may have ElderShield instead.
➕
Supplements from private insurers can top up the base payout amount
💳
Payouts can offset your net monthly care cost if your father qualifies and has an active policy
What to check
Does Father have CareShield Life? Check with CPF Board
Born before 1980? May have ElderShield — check MOH
To check policy status cpf.gov.sg/careshield
If your father has an active CareShield Life or ElderShield policy and currently cannot perform ≥3 ADLs, payouts may already be due. Contact CPF Board to initiate a disability assessment claim.

📚
Caregivers Training Grant
Subsidises approved training courses to improve caregiving skills
Up to S$200 / year
Per caregiver · No income cap · Immediate family and live-in caregivers
Eligibility
✅
Caring for a person with disability or chronic illness — no age requirement for care recipient
✅
No household income cap — available to all income levels
✅
Eligible caregivers: immediate family members, non-family live-in caregivers
How to use it
1️⃣
Find an AIC-approved training course on aic.sg/ctg (caregiving skills, dementia care, first aid)
2️⃣
Register and attend — the subsidy is applied directly; no upfront claim needed
3️⃣
Up to S$200/year per caregiver — courses typically cost S$50–S$150 each
📅
Course intake: Oct 2026 – Mar 2027Next window opens in ~4 months. We'll remind you in September.
